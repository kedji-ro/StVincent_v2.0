History

St. Vincent Strambi C.P of Home for the Aged

With the common desire to help and extend pastoral works, the concerted efforts of the different civic and religious movements, generous individuals, and families were conceived and concretized the Home for the Aged on November 7, 1991, which marked the groundbreaking ceremony.

Mission and Vision

Our Mission

We commit ourselves to provide integrated services for the upliftment of the general well-being of the aged.

Our Vision

We, the St, Vincent Strambi Home for the Aged, Inc. officers, board members, and personnel founded on the Passionist tradition of sharing the life of the crucified One and the crucified ones in the society, envision a quality Residential Care Program to the elderly people ages 60 and above who are indigent, neglected and abandoned.

Programs and Services

St. Vincent Strambi Home for the Aged, Inc.

It is good to note that St. Vincent Strambi Home for the Aged, Inc. is within the guidance of the Social Service Ministry of the Catholic Women's League (CWL). It is dedicated to take part in uplifting the life and well-being of the indigent and to help them become responsible individuals. It has specific services addressed to disadvantaged individual or group in society to nourish their well-being towards becoming functional and productive persons. In ensuring the holistic development of the clients, support services are being developed to fit with their realities and needs.

Dictionary defines Program as a plan to actions for achieving something or a set of activities with specific goal. It is a system of procedures or activities that has a specific purpose. Further, dictionary defines Services as set of action to be done to help or facilitate improvement to someone or to a set of people within a given situation.

The programs and services are being presented according to description and scope to assist the Administrative Council and the concerned personnel in understanding the details of program operations.

CORE PROGRAM AND SERVICES

Core Program refers the primary interventions that are being facilitated in the healing and move, process of a particular client.

St. Vincent Strambi Home for the Aged, Inc. as it continues to facilitate the development and implementation of helping interventions for its clientele identifies it main program with corresponding services. These are Residential Care Program.

Residential Care Program

This program ensures facilitation of holistic residential care for the clients. The key elements in Residential Care Program are effective and efficient case management and quality home-life care and management. In recognition of the mandate from the Department of Social Welfare and Development (DSWD), it is essential that Residential Care in charge is a licensed social worker.

Beyond the prima, purpose to ensure compliance to the DS WD Standards, the licensed Social Worker is equally responsible for the development of the prop-am and related services. These programs and services should ultimately contribute to the client's further rehabilitation and healing towards harnessing their innate capacity to live their lives.

Among others, it includes the provision of temporary shelter. home-life care. health and Medical, Social, Occupational, Therapeutic and Occupation Services and Mortuary services. Essentially. these are considered as the pipeline for the rehabilitation and treatment towards the continuous growth and development of the clients.

Home Life Care Services and Management

It is a primarily concern about the total care and guidance of the client inside a particular institution. The totality of the client responses to the whole homelife environment are of greatest value in the homelife care. These include the client behavior in terms of relationship with others especially with fellow client, institutional responsibility such as care for personal and institutional belongings and relationship with God.

SUPPORT SERVICES

St. Vincent Strambi Home for the Aged, inc. identifies the support services encompassing the needed interventions for the core program. These are necessary in the rehabilitation and development of its clientele towards enabling them to effect change and be able to live their own lives as empowered persons with conscientious responsibility.

Social and Recreational Services

Social and Recreational Services are enabling services rendered in a more positive approach in helping the elderly to adjust to their interpersonal losses (loss of work, roles and bereavement and social isolation) and assist them in the development of new social roles and meaningful use of their time to uplift their sense of belongingness and to be recognize as a member in the community.

These social and recreational activities implement with the help of our student trainees, visitors and it includes elderly birthdays, anniversary. Christmas, New Year, Valentine's celebration, and OJT farewell party.

Health and Medical Services

The centers promote and provide basic nursing home care with the help of our On-the-Job Training caregivers, Health Care Services, Midwifery and Nursing students. The elderly also assisted in providing general care such as bathing. grooming and in maintaining their general hygiene.

This includes consultation and treatment, referral for special physical examination and hospitalization, the issuance of interpretation of medical consultation, laboratory request and other medical reports.

The medical need of the residents is king supervised by the volunteer physicians on either monthly or quarterly basis. It includes free medical consultation. provision medicine, and physical therapy sessions among others. Daily monitoring and recording of the resident vital signs is likewise undertaken.

For the residents who have contagious illness, the center provides an infirmary area to isolate them from others. However, the daily intake of medicine and monitoring of his/her medical needs is being monitored by the staff and Off students assigned to the respective elderly. Proper recording is undertaken to ensure that the medical needs of the clients are strictly attended.

Physical Restoration and Therapeutic Services

It provides opportunities for the elderly to engage in various activities that would make them productive and to regain self-esteem with the help of our volunteers. OJT caregivers, midwifery, health care services and nursing students.

Spiritual Services

Has the fundamental responsibility to form Me faith-life of the clients to ensure their spiritual growth

Mortuary Services

These services are provided to the residents once they already reach his/her final destination.

The following activities are undertaken:

* Taking care of remain of residents
* Inform the family and relatives (if there's any) for possible claiming or body.
* Coordinate with the City Social Welfare and Development Offices (CSWDO) for the burial assistance.
* Coordinate with the funeral; pay the corresponding funeral charges, cemetery staff in the preparation of the grave.
* Facilitate / coordinate with the priest for the necessary mass for the soul of the dead residents.
